GSE68086 — Tumor-Educated Platelets (Best et al., Cancer Cell 2015)

Domain: Blood-platelet RNA-seq, pan-cancer liquid biopsy (285 samples, 6 cancer types + healthy; 57,736 genes) Generated: 2026-06-23

The canonical blood-platelet liquid-biopsy benchmark, heavily reused yet not previously audited at the raw-data level. Automated data-quality audit of the raw GEO deposit finds the dataset largely clean — no high-impact defect. One genuine data-entry error: cell_type is stored under two spellings — Thrombocytes (×245) and thrombocytes (×40) — a case-split that silently fragments the category in any group-by (confirmed in the raw column; the lowercase rows fall in one submission batch). The remaining items are real but expected-by-design: severe zero-inflation (85% zeros, with zeros denoting below-detection), batch⊥cancer-type entanglement (Batch01 is GBM-only and cancer types are not randomized across runs — a confounder for naive class comparisons), a single within-patient replicate (Lung-20 / Lung-20-1), and singleton mutation subtypes. Every candidate was checked against the deposited matrix before listing.
